Privacy Policy

Oxford Dictionary Lookup Browser Extension

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

NiftyBox ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Oxford Dictionary Lookup browser extension ("the Extension") handles your information. By using the Extension, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be transparent about what information is collected and how it is used:

2.1 Information Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following information locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage):

  • User Preferences: Your settings such as selected dictionary version (UK/US), theme preference (dark/light mode), and UI customization options
  • Lookup History (Optional): If enabled in settings, recently looked-up words for quick access. This data never leaves your device

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does NOT:

  • Collect, transmit, or store your personal information
  • Track your browsing activity or history
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Send any analytics or telemetry data
  • Access your email, contacts, or other personal data

3. How We Use Information

All information stored by the Extension is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Functionality: To provide dictionary lookup functionality and display definitions
  • User Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences across sessions
  • User Experience: To maintain lookup history (if enabled) for your convenience

All data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services, except for the dictionary lookup requests sent directly to Oxford Learner's Dictionary website.

4. Third-Party Services

When you look up a word, the Extension sends requests to the Oxford Learner's Dictionary website (owned and operated by Oxford University Press) to retrieve dictionary definitions. These requests are subject to Oxford University Press's own privacy policy and terms of service.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Oxford University Press. We do not control or have access to any information collected by Oxford University Press through your use of their website.

5. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. All data stored by the Extension is kept locally on your device using browser storage mechanisms, which are isolated from other extensions and websites. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by the Extension remains on your device until:

  • You manually clear it using the Extension's settings
  • You clear your browser's storage/cache
  • You uninstall the Extension

Uninstalling the Extension will automatically delete all locally stored data associated with it from your device.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can access all stored data through the Extension's settings or your browser's developer tools
  • Deletion: You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the Extension's storage or uninstalling it
  • Portability: You can export your data by accessing browser storage
  • Control: You have full control over what features you enable and what data is stored

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Extension after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • By visiting our GitHub repository: https://github.com/niftybox/ox-lookup-support
  • By opening an issue in our support repository with your privacy-related questions
